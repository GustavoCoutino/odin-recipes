Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a savory Italian dish renowned for its layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty and comforting dish is a culinary masterpiece that brings together textures and flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or boiled)
  • Ground beef, ground pork, or a mix of both (or substitute with vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk)
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce
  3. Assemble the Lasagna
  4. Finish Assembling
  5. Baking
  6. Serve
