Taco

Description

These classic beef tacos are a delicious and satisfying option for a quick and flavorful meal. Seasoned ground beef is nestled in soft corn tortillas and topped with a vibrant array of toppings, creating a perfect balance of textures and flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup beef broth or water

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef, breaking it up with a spatula as it cooks, until it's no longer pink.
  2. Add the chopped onion to the beef and cook for a few more minutes until the onion is translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic, chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook for another minute until the spices are fragrant.
  4. Pour in the beef broth or water, and let the mixture simmer for about 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the liquid to reduce. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  5. While the beef filling is simmering, warm the corn tortillas. You can do this by heating them briefly in a dry skillet or in the oven wrapped in aluminum foil.
  6. To assemble each taco, place a spoonful of the seasoned beef filling onto the center of a corn tortilla.
  7. Top the beef filling with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, grated cheddar cheese, a dollop of sour cream, and sliced jalape√±os if desired.
  8. Garnish the tacos with chopped fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the tacos.
  9. Repeat the assembly process for the remaining tortillas.
  10. Serve the classic beef tacos immediately while they're still warm and all the toppings are fresh.
